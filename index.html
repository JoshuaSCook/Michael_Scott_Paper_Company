Mission Statement

Michael Scott Paper Company Inc. is an innovative wholesale packaging distributor, whose goal is to supply clients not only with a wide array of products - quickly, efficiently and cost-effectively - but also to offer creative solutions to fill any particular needs you may have.

For more than 3 weeks, Michael Scott Paper has served clients throughout Scranton, PA where we provide an assortment of wholesale paper supplies. Michael Scott Paper provides each client, from school systems, to government agencies, from office environments to tech start-ups, with customized paper solutions.

We are your single source for all of your paper needs, consultative service, and total customer satisfaction!
